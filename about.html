Built by Collectors, for Collectors

DexVault was born from a simple frustration: managing a Pokémon card collection shouldn't require three spreadsheets and five different apps.

Our Mission

We believe every collector — from the casual fan with 50 cards to the serious investor with thousands — deserves professional-grade tools. DexVault brings together scanning, pricing, grading, trading, and analytics in one beautifully designed platform.

We're obsessed with the details: accurate AI scanning, real-time pricing, and a community that actually helps each other.

🎯 Accuracy First

Our AI is trained on millions of cards. We don't ship until it's right.

💎 Collector-Centric

Every feature is designed by collectors who use it daily.

🔒 Trust & Safety

Escrow-protected trades and verified listings keep the community safe.
